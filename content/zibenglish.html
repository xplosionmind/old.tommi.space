Zibaldone

Zibaldone di Pensieri is a great collection of notes by the great italian author Giacomo Leopardi. These writings don’t have even a tiny bit of profoundness, power and wit of the original collection. Just a touch of glee, craziness of my everyday life.
Translating the definition by Treccani encyclopedia, żibaldóne: […] Notebook where unordered news, notes, thoughts, quotes, schemes, sketches are noted.
Exactly that.
